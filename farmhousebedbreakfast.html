Farmhouse bed & breakfast at Cwm Craig Farm

Cwm Craig is a working farm offering 4 star farmhouse bed & breakfast accommodation in rural Herefordshire.

Welcome to Cwm Craig, a 19th century Georgian  farmhouse on a working farm,set in 150 acres of the beautiful Wye Valley, an area of outstanding natural beauty.

Cwm Craig is a family run business that is open all year except Christmas and New Year.We offer a warm welcome with comfortable spacious accomodation.Our aim is to provide a relaxing environment for all our guests.

A traditional home cooked breakfast is served in the large dining room and guests also have access to a sitting room and games room, which has a top of the range pool table and a darts board.

There are many pubs and restaurants within a few miles of Cwm Craig where guests can enjoy a delicious evening meal.

We have excellent facilities and very reasonable prices

Andrea Lee and the family look forward to welcoming you.
